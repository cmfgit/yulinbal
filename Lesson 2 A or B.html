DNA Quick Quiz

Stand on the side of the room that matches your answer!

Question 1

What does DNA stand for?

A

Deoxyribonucleic Acid

B

Different Nucleotide Arrangement

Answer: A

DNA stands for Deoxyribonucleic Acid

Question 2

In our lolly DNA model, what did the different colored lollies represent?

A

Different genes

B

Different nucleotide bases

Answer: B

The different colored lollies represented the four different nucleotide bases (A, T, G, C)

Question 3

Which bases pair together in DNA?

A

A pairs with G, and T pairs with C

B

A pairs with T, and G pairs with C

Answer: B

A pairs with T, and G pairs with C

This is called complementary base pairing

Question 4

What is a nucleotide?

A

The small building block (monomer) that makes up DNA

B

A complete section of DNA that controls one trait

Answer: A

A nucleotide is a monomer - the small building block that makes up DNA

Question 5

DNA is a polymer. What does "poly" mean?

A

Many

B

Large

Answer: A

"Poly" means many

Polymer = many units joined together

Question 6

Do all living organisms contain DNA?

A

No, only animals have DNA

B

Yes, all living organisms contain DNA

Answer: B

Yes! All living organisms - from bacteria to blue whales to banana plants - contain DNA

Question 7

In our lolly model, if one side of the DNA had the sequence A-T-G-C, what would the other side be?

A

T-A-C-G

B

G-C-A-T

Answer: A

T-A-C-G

Remember: A pairs with T, and G pairs with C!

Great Work!

  • ✓ Nucleotides are the building blocks of DNA
  • ✓ Complementary base pairing (A-T and G-C)
