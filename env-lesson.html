35-Minute Lesson Plan

Unit: Environmental Change and Management

Inquiry Question: How do environments support human life?

Year Level: Geography

5 minutes

Introduction

  • Show title slide and introduce inquiry question
  • Think-Pair-Share: "What environments have you visited?"
  • Quick brainstorm: Students call out environment types (record on board)
10 minutes

Direct Instruction

  • Present slides 2-5: Define environments, types, Australian environments
  • Students take notes in their workbooks
  • Interactive questions: "Who can name an Australian desert?" etc.
8 minutes

Case Study & Video

  • Show slide 6: Amazon Rainforest case study
  • Play video on Amazon's role in climate regulation (5 min)
  • Students annotate: Record 3 key facts about climate regulation
  • Guided questions: "How does the Amazon produce oxygen?" "Why is it important globally?"
8 minutes

Mapping Activity

  • Display Australian map with satellite imagery
  • Interactive demonstration: Identify major environments by color/location
  • Students complete worksheet: Label 5 major Australian environments
  • Use topographic maps to identify elevation and features
3 minutes

Graphic Organizer

  • Show slide 7: How environments support human life
  • Students create quick graphic organizer with 5 ways (food, water, air, resources, climate)
4 minutes

Conclusion & Discussion

  • Whole class discussion: Return to inquiry question
  • Students share one way environments support their daily life
  • Preview next lesson: Environmental threats and management
  • Optional: Assign quiz as homework or use as formative assessment
