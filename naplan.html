Important information for Year 9 students, parents and carers

What is NAPLAN?

The National Assessment Program – Literacy and Numeracy (NAPLAN) is Australia's only national assessment, undertaken by students in Years 3, 5, 7 and 9 each year. It assesses the literacy and numeracy skills students are learning through the school curriculum.

NAPLAN is just one aspect of a school's assessment process. It does not replace ongoing teacher assessments, but provides additional information about your child's educational progress against national proficiency standards.

Results are reported across four proficiency levels: Exceeding, Strong, Developing and Needs additional support.

Online Adaptive Testing

Year 9 students complete all NAPLAN tests online. The tests are tailored (adaptive) — questions may become more or less difficult depending on a student's responses, allowing a wider range of abilities to be measured more precisely.

Students should not be concerned if they find questions challenging — they may simply be on a more complex test pathway. Results are based on both the number and complexity of questions answered correctly.

How to Support Your Child

Students are not expected to study for NAPLAN. The best support is to encourage your child to simply do their best. Teachers will ensure students are familiar with the types of questions and provide appropriate guidance.

ACARA does not recommend excessive preparation or the use of coaching services for NAPLAN. If you'd like your child to see the types of questions asked, visit the NAPLAN Public Demonstration Site.

⚠️ Exemptions from NAPLAN

Action Required

In exceptional circumstances, a formal exemption from NAPLAN may be available — for example, for a student with a disability that severely limits their capacity to participate, or a student who has recently arrived in Australia with a non-English speaking background.

Students may also be withdrawn from the test at the request of the parent/carer. Parents/carers must contact the school office immediately to complete the required forms.
